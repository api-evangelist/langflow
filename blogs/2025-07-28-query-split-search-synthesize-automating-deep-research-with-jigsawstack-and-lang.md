While platforms like Perplexity and OpenAI offer deep research through closed systems, developers can now build their own transparent, extensible research agents using JigsawStack's open-source framework within Langflow. This hands-on guide shows how to create a multi-step research pipeline that breaks down complex queries, performs iterative web searches, and compiles evidence-backed reports—all through visual workflows that accelerate development without writing code. Learn to prototype AI research agents that you can see, modify, and scale for your specific needs.
